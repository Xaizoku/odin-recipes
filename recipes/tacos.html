Tacos

The Dish

Tacos are a traditional Mexican dish composed of a corn or wheat tortilla folded or rolled around a filling.

Fillings can include beef, pork, chicken, seafood, vegetables, and cheese.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1/2 cup diced onion
  • 1/2 cup diced tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup diced green bell pepper
  • 1/4 cup diced red bell pepper
  • 1/4 cup diced yellow bell pepper
  • 1/4 cup diced green onions
  • 1/4 cup salsa
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • 1/4 cup taco seasoning mix
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 8 (6 inch) flour tortillas
  • 1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 cup sour cream

Directions

  1. In a large skillet over medium heat, cook the ground beef until evenly brown.
  2. Drain, and mix in onion, tomatoes, bell peppers, green onions, and salsa.
  3. Season with cilantro, taco seasoning mix, and water.
  4. Simmer until vegetables are tender.
  5. Wrap tortillas in a damp cloth and microwave on high for 1 minute, or until heated through.
  6. Spoon equal amounts of the mixture into each tortilla.
  7. Top with Cheddar cheese and sour cream.
